About				: OSMI Mental Health in Tech Survey 2016
Description			: This project leverages the OSMI Mental Health in Tech Survey 2016,
				  dataset to examine the prevalence of mental health challenges and attitudes 
				  toward treatment among tech workers. 
				  The focus is on identifying barriers to care and opportunities for workplace intervention.
Data Source			: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/osmi/mental-health-in-tech-2016
Target Column(s) for Analysis	: Seek Treatment (Binary: Yes/No)
